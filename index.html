About

Where Orchestral Meets Intimate

Matt Roberts is a composer and music producer based in Southwest Florida. He writes orchestral compositions that cover a wide range — from epic cinematic scores to quiet, reflective worship pieces.

His approach blends traditional orchestral arranging with modern production tools, and his work has appeared in ministry film productions and game soundtracks. He handles everything from initial concept to final mix.

Outside of music, Matt works in construction technology — but composing is the thing that keeps pulling him back to the desk late at night.
